PRIVACY AND TELEMETRY
---------------------

WinFast Pro X does not collect, send or transmit any personal data,
device identifiers, usage information or telemetry of any kind to any
remote server. All processing — including the Smart Advisor — runs
exclusively on the user's local device. The Software requires no account,
